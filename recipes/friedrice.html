Fried Rice

Fried rice is a staple of Asian cuisine. Typically it is cooked using leftover rice, as the rice has been dehydrated and cooled down so the heat doesn't overcook it. A simple dish, it serves as a way to cook in any leftover rice with whatever ingredients lie within the fridge from previous days.

Ingredients

  • Leftover Rice
  • Frozen Mixed Vegetables
  • Eggs
  • Shrimp

Cooking Instructions

  1. Fry the eggs first and scramble
  2. Add frozen vegetables until hot
  3. Stir in leftover rice
  4. Add in 1 part light soy sauce, 1 part dark soy sauce
  5. Add shrimp to the mix and cook until pink
  6. Remove from heat and serve

Etouffee

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Holy Trinity (pepper, onions, celery)
  • Shrimp
  • Chicken Broth

Cooking Instructions

  1. Make roux using butter and flour. Cook until brown
  2. Add in holy trinity and cook for 5 min
  3. Add in chicken broth and cajun seasoning
  4. Cook shrimp in the stew until fully cooked
  5. Remove from heat and serve over rice

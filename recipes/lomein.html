Lo Mein

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Mixed Frozen Vegetables
  • Shrimp

Cooking Instructions

  1. Boil noodles for 4 min
  2. Heat up mixed frozen vegetables in pan
  3. Add noodles to pan and heat up
  4. Add 1 part light soy sauce, 1 part dark soy sauce
  5. Add shrimp and cook until pink
  6. Remove from heat and serve
